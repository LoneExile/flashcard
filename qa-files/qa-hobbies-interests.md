# Hobbies/Interests Q&A

## What Are Your Hobbies (ài hào)

### Questions:
- nǐ xǐ huān zuò shén me? - What do you like to do?
- nǐ de ài hào shì shén me? - What are your hobbies?
- nǐ píng shí xǐ huān zuò shén me? - What do you usually like to do?
- nǐ zhōu mò xǐ huān zuò shén me? - What do you like to do on weekends?
- tā xǐ huān zuò shén me? - What does he/she like to do?

### Answers:
- wǒ xǐ huān pá shān - I like hiking/climbing mountains
- wǒ xǐ huān sàn bù - I like taking walks
- wǒ xǐ huān wán yóu xì - I like playing games
- wǒ xǐ huān tīng gē - I like listening to music
- wǒ xǐ huān kàn diàn yǐng - I like watching movies
- wǒ xǐ huān kàn diàn shì - I like watching TV
- wǒ xǐ huān kàn shū - I like reading books
- wǒ xǐ huān guàng jiē - I like shopping/window shopping

## Hiking/Walking (pá shān / sàn bù)

### Questions:
- nǐ xǐ huān pá shān ma? - Do you like hiking?
- nǐ píng shí pá shān ma? - Do you usually go hiking?
- nǐ hé shéi yì qǐ pá shān? - Who do you go hiking with?
- nǐ píng shí hé shéi pá shān? - Who do you usually go hiking with?
- nǐ zhōu mò qù pá shān ma? - Do you go hiking on weekends?

### Answers:
- wǒ xǐ huān pá shān - I like hiking
- wǒ fēi cháng xǐ huān pá shān - I really like hiking
- wǒ píng shí hé wǒ jiě jie pá shān - I usually go hiking with my older sister
- wǒ hé péng yǒu yì qǐ pá shān - I go hiking with friends
- wǒ zhōu mò qù gōng yuán sàn bù - I go for walks in the park on weekends
- wǒ xǐ huān zài gōng yuán sàn bù - I like taking walks in the park
