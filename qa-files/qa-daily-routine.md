# Daily Routine Q&A

## Wake Up (qǐ chuáng)

### Questions:
- nǐ jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time do you wake up?
- nǐ zǎo shàng jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time do you wake up in the morning?
- nǐ shàng wǔ jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time do you wake up in the morning?
- tā jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time does he/she wake up?
- nǐ de mèi mei jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time does your younger sister wake up?

### Answers:
- wǒ liù diǎn qǐ chuáng - I wake up at 6 o'clock
- wǒ zǎo shàng qī diǎn qǐ chuáng - I wake up at 7 in the morning
- wǒ shàng wǔ liù diǎn bàn qǐ chuáng - I wake up at 6:30 in the morning
- tā bā diǎn qǐ chuáng - He/She wakes up at 8 o'clock
- wǒ de mèi mei qī diǎn shí wǔ fēn qǐ chuáng - My younger sister wakes up at 7:15

## Time Expressions

### Exact Time (jǐ diǎn)
- liǎng diǎn - 2 o'clock (Note: use liǎng, not èr)
- sān diǎn shí fēn - 3:10
- sì diǎn shí wǔ fēn - 4:15
- wǔ diǎn èr shí fēn - 5:20
- liù diǎn sān shí fēn / liù diǎn bàn - 6:30
- qī diǎn sì shí wǔ fēn - 7:45
- bā diǎn wǔ shí fēn - 8:50
- jiǔ diǎn líng wǔ fēn - 9:05

### Time Periods
- zǎo shàng / shàng wǔ - morning
- zhōng wǔ - noon
- xià wǔ - afternoon
- wǎn shàng - evening

### Time with Context
- shàng wǔ bā diǎn - 8 AM (morning)
- xià wǔ sān diǎn - 3 PM (afternoon)
- wǎn shàng jiǔ diǎn - 9 PM (evening)
- zhōng wǔ shí èr diǎn - 12 noon

## Weekend Routine (zhōu mò rì cháng)

### Questions:
- nǐ zhōu mò jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time do you wake up on weekends?
- nǐ xīng qī liù jǐ diǎn qǐ chuáng? - What time do you wake up on Saturday?
- nǐ zhōu rì zuò shén me? - What do you do on Sunday?

### Answers:
- wǒ zhōu mò jiǔ diǎn qǐ chuáng - I wake up at 9 on weekends
- wǒ xīng qī liù shí diǎn qǐ chuáng - I wake up at 10 on Saturday
- wǒ zhōu rì zài jiā xiū xi - I rest at home on Sunday
- wǒ zhōu mò bù shàng bān - I don't work on weekends
