# Family Details Q&A

## Mother's Name and Details (mā ma de xìn xī)

### Questions:
- nǐ de mā ma jiào shén me míng zì? - What is your mother's name?
- nǐ mā ma jiào shén me? - What is your mother's name?
- nǐ de mā ma jīn nián duō dà le? - How old is your mother this year?
- nǐ mā ma duō dà? - How old is your mother?
- nǐ de mā ma zuò shén me gōng zuò? - What is your mother's job?
- nǐ de mā ma zài nǎ lǐ gōng zuò? - Where does your mother work?
- nǐ de mā ma zhù zài nǎ lǐ? - Where does your mother live?

### Answers:
- wǒ de mā ma jiào [name] - My mother's name is [name]
- wǒ mā ma jīn nián wǔ shí suì - My mother is 50 years old this year
- wǒ de mā ma shì yī shēng - My mother is a doctor
- wǒ de mā ma shì lǎo shī - My mother is a teacher
- wǒ de mā ma zài yī yuàn gōng zuò - My mother works at a hospital
- wǒ de mā ma zhù zài běi jīng - My mother lives in Beijing
- wǒ mā ma zài xiāng gǎng gōng zuò - My mother works in Hong Kong
