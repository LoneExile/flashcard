# Plans and Activities Q&A

## Plans for Today (jīn tiān de jì huà)

### Questions:
- nǐ jīn tiān zuò shén me? - What are you doing today?
- jīn tiān nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing today?
- nǐ jīn tiān xiǎng zuò shén me? - What do you want to do today?
- jīn tiān xià wǔ nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing this afternoon?
- jīn tiān wǎn shàng nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing tonight?
- nǐ jīn tiān qù xué xiào ma? - Are you going to school today?
- nǐ jīn tiān shàng bān ma? - Are you going to work today?

### Answers:
- jīn tiān wǒ qù xué xiào - Today I go to school
- wǒ jīn tiān shàng bān - I go to work today
- jīn tiān wǒ zài jiā xiū xi - Today I rest at home
- wǒ jīn tiān kàn shū - I read books today
- jīn tiān xià wǔ wǒ qù gōng yuán sàn bù - This afternoon I go to the park for a walk
- jīn tiān wǎn shàng wǒ kàn diàn shì - Tonight I watch TV
- wǒ jīn tiān xiǎng qù shāng diàn mǎi dōng xī - Today I want to go to the store to buy things

## Plans for Tomorrow (míng tiān de jì huà)

### Questions:
- nǐ míng tiān zuò shén me? - What are you doing tomorrow?
- míng tiān nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing tomorrow?
- nǐ míng tiān xiǎng zuò shén me? - What do you want to do tomorrow?
- míng tiān shàng wǔ nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing tomorrow morning?
- míng tiān zǎo shàng nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing tomorrow morning?
- nǐ míng tiān qù nǎ lǐ? - Where are you going tomorrow?
- nǐ míng tiān shàng bān ma? - Are you going to work tomorrow?

### Answers:
- míng tiān wǒ qù xué xiào shàng kè - Tomorrow I go to school to have class
- wǒ míng tiān qù gōng sī - Tomorrow I go to the company
- míng tiān wǒ zài jiā xiū xi - Tomorrow I rest at home
- wǒ míng tiān xiǎng qù gōng yuán - Tomorrow I want to go to the park
- míng tiān shàng wǔ wǒ shàng bān - Tomorrow morning I go to work
- míng tiān zǎo shàng wǒ shàng bān - Tomorrow morning I go to work
- míng tiān wǒ xiǎng qù cān tīng chī fàn - Tomorrow I want to go to a restaurant to eat
- wǒ míng tiān bù shàng bān - I don't go to work tomorrow

## What You Did Yesterday (zuó tiān zuò le shén me)

### Questions:
- nǐ zuó tiān zuò le shén me? - What did you do yesterday?
- zuó tiān nǐ zuò le shén me? - What did you do yesterday?
- nǐ zuó tiān qù nǎ lǐ le? - Where did you go yesterday?
- zuó tiān xià wǔ nǐ zuò le shén me? - What did you do yesterday afternoon?
- zuó tiān wǎn shàng nǐ zuò le shén me? - What did you do yesterday evening?
- zuó wǎn nǐ zuò le shén me? - What did you do last night?
- nǐ zuó tiān qù xué xiào le ma? - Did you go to school yesterday?
- nǐ zuó tiān mǎi dōng xī le ma? - Did you buy things yesterday?

### Answers:
- zuó tiān wǒ qù xué xiào le - Yesterday I went to school
- wǒ zuó tiān qù gōng yuán sàn bù le - Yesterday I went to the park for a walk
- zuó tiān wǒ zài jiā kàn shū - Yesterday I read books at home
- wǒ zuó tiān qù shāng diàn mǎi dōng xī le - Yesterday I went to the store to buy things
- zuó tiān xià wǔ wǒ qù guàng jiē le - Yesterday afternoon I went shopping
- zuó wǎn wǒ kàn diàn shì - Last night I watched TV
- zuó wǎn wǒ zài jiā kàn diàn yǐng - Last night I watched a movie at home
- wǒ zuó tiān méi qù xué xiào - I didn't go to school yesterday
- wǒ zuó tiān méi mǎi dōng xī - I didn't buy things yesterday

## Plans for This Weekend (zhè ge zhōu mò de jì huà)

### Questions:
- nǐ zhè ge zhōu mò zuò shén me? - What are you doing this weekend?
- zhè ge zhōu mò nǐ xiǎng zuò shén me? - What do you want to do this weekend?
- nǐ zhè zhōu mò qù nǎ lǐ? - Where are you going this weekend?
- zhè ge xīng qī liù nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing this Saturday?
- zhè ge xīng qī tiān nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing this Sunday?
- zhè ge zhōu rì nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing this Sunday?
- nǐ zhè zhōu mò xiǎng qù gōng yuán ma? - Do you want to go to the park this weekend?

### Answers:
- zhè ge zhōu mò wǒ xiǎng xiū xi - This weekend I want to rest
- wǒ zhè zhōu mò qù gōng yuán - This weekend I go to the park
- zhè ge xīng qī liù wǒ qù guàng jiē - This Saturday I go shopping
- zhè ge xīng qī tiān wǒ zài jiā kàn shū - This Sunday I read books at home
- zhè ge zhōu rì wǒ qù cān tīng chī fàn - This Sunday I go to a restaurant to eat
- wǒ zhè zhōu mò xiǎng qù shāng diàn mǎi yì xiē shuǐ guǒ - This weekend I want to go to the store to buy some fruit
- zhè zhōu mò wǒ bù xiǎng shàng bān - This weekend I don't want to work

## What You Did Last Sunday (shàng ge zhōu rì zuò le shén me)

### Questions:
- nǐ shàng ge zhōu rì zuò le shén me? - What did you do last Sunday?
- shàng ge xīng qī tiān nǐ zuò le shén me? - What did you do last Sunday?
- nǐ shàng ge zhōu mò zuò le shén me? - What did you do last weekend?
- shàng zhōu mò nǐ qù nǎ lǐ le? - Where did you go last weekend?
- nǐ shàng ge zhōu liù zuò le shén me? - What did you do last Saturday?
- shàng ge xīng qī liù nǐ zuò le shén me? - What did you do last Saturday?

### Answers:
- shàng ge zhōu rì wǒ qù gōng yuán le - Last Sunday I went to the park
- wǒ shàng ge xīng qī tiān zài jiā xiū xi - Last Sunday I rested at home
- shàng ge zhōu mò wǒ qù guàng jiē le - Last weekend I went shopping
- wǒ shàng zhōu mò kàn diàn yǐng le - Last weekend I watched a movie
- shàng ge zhōu liù wǒ qù cān tīng chī fàn le - Last Saturday I went to a restaurant to eat
- shàng ge zhōu mò wǒ hé péng yǒu yì qǐ sàn bù - Last weekend I took a walk with friends
- wǒ shàng ge zhōu rì méi zuò shén me - I didn't do anything last Sunday

## Negative Forms - Not Going/Not Doing

### Questions:
- nǐ bù qù xué xiào ma? - Don't you go to school?
- nǐ bù xiǎng qù gōng yuán ma? - Don't you want to go to the park?
- nǐ bù shàng bān ma? - Don't you go to work?
- nǐ bù xiǎng chī fàn ma? - Don't you want to eat?

### Answers:
- wǒ bù qù xué xiào - I don't go to school
- wǒ bù xiǎng shàng bān - I don't want to go to work
- wǒ jīn tiān bù xiǎng qù gōng yuán - Today I don't want to go to the park
- wǒ míng tiān bù shàng kè - Tomorrow I don't have class
- wǒ bù xiǎng qù cān tīng chī fàn - I don't want to go to a restaurant to eat

## Time Expressions - Morning/Afternoon/Evening

### Morning (zǎo shàng / shàng wǔ)
- jīn tiān zǎo shàng wǒ qù xué xiào - This morning I go to school
- jīn tiān shàng wǔ wǒ shàng bān - This morning I go to work
- míng tiān zǎo shàng wǒ xiū xi - Tomorrow morning I rest
- míng tiān shàng wǔ wǒ kàn shū - Tomorrow morning I read books

### Afternoon (xià wǔ)
- jīn tiān xià wǔ wǒ qù gōng yuán - This afternoon I go to the park
- míng tiān xià wǔ wǒ xiǎng mǎi dōng xī - Tomorrow afternoon I want to buy things
- zuó tiān xià wǔ wǒ qù guàng jiē le - Yesterday afternoon I went shopping

### Evening (wǎn shàng)
- jīn tiān wǎn shàng wǒ kàn diàn shì - Tonight I watch TV
- míng tiān wǎn shàng wǒ xiǎng kàn diàn yǐng - Tomorrow night I want to watch a movie
- zuó wǎn wǒ zài jiā xiū xi - Last night I rested at home

## Next Week Plans (xià zhōu / xià ge xīng qī)

### Questions:
- nǐ xià zhōu zuò shén me? - What are you doing next week?
- xià ge xīng qī yī nǐ zuò shén me? - What are you doing next Monday?
- nǐ xià zhōu qù nǎ lǐ? - Where are you going next week?

### Answers:
- xià zhōu wǒ qù shàng hǎi - Next week I go to Shanghai
- xià ge xīng qī yī wǒ shàng bān - Next Monday I go to work
- wǒ xià zhōu qù běi jīng chū chāi - Next week I go to Beijing on a business trip
- xià ge zhōu mò wǒ xiǎng xiū xi - Next weekend I want to rest
