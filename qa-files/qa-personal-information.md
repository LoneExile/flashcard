# Personal Information Q&A

## Name (míng zì)

### Questions:
- nǐ jiào shén me míng zì? - What is your name?
- qǐng wèn nǐ jiào shén me míng zì? - May I ask what is your name?
- tā jiào shén me míng zì? - What is his/her name?
- nǐ de lǎo shī jiào shén me míng zì? - What is your teacher's name?

### Answers:
- wǒ jiào [name] - My name is [name]
- tā jiào [name] - His/Her name is [name]
- wǒ de lǎo shī jiào [name] - My teacher's name is [name]

## Age (nián líng)

### Questions:
- nǐ jīn nián duō dà le? - How old are you this year?
- nǐ duō dà? - How old are you?
- tā jīn nián duō dà le? - How old is he/she this year?
- nǐ de mèi mei jīn nián duō dà le? - How old is your younger sister this year?

### Answers:
- wǒ jīn nián èr shí wǔ suì - I am 25 years old this year
- wǒ sān shí suì le - I am 30 years old
- tā jīn nián shí bā suì - He/She is 18 years old this year

## Nationality (guó jí)

### Questions:
- nǐ shì nǎ guó rén? - What nationality are you?
- nǐ shì zhōng guó rén ma? - Are you Chinese?
- tā shì měi guó rén ma? - Is he/she American?
- nǐ shì bú shì rì běn rén? - Are you Japanese?

### Answers:
- wǒ shì zhōng guó rén - I am Chinese
- wǒ shì měi guó rén - I am American
- wǒ bú shì rì běn rén - I am not Japanese
- tā shì yīng guó rén - He/She is British
- wǒ shì tài guó rén - I am Thai
- tā bú shì dé guó rén - He/She is not German
- wǒ shì fǎ guó rén - I am French

### Answers:

- wǒ lái zì fǎ guó - I come from France
- wǒ de lǎo shī lái zì shàng hǎi - My teacher comes from Shanghai

## Family Members (jiā rén)

### Questions:
- nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén? - How many people are in your family?
- nǐ yǒu gē ge ma? - Do you have an older brother?
- nǐ yǒu jiě jie ma? - Do you have an older sister?
- nǐ yǒu dì di mèi mei ma? - Do you have younger siblings?

### Answers:
- wǒ jiā yǒu sì kǒu rén - My family has four people
- wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén - My family has five people
- wǒ yǒu yī gè gē ge - I have one older brother
- wǒ yǒu liǎng gè mèi mei - I have two younger sisters
- wǒ méi yǒu dì di - I don't have a younger brother

## Job (gōng zuò)

### Questions:
- nǐ zuò shén me gōng zuò? - What is your job?
- nǐ shì xué shēng ma? - Are you a student?
- tā zuò shén me gōng zuò? - What is his/her job?
- nǐ de bà ba zuò shén me gōng zuò? - What is your father's job?
- nǐ de mā ma shì yī shēng ma? - Is your mother a doctor?

### Answers:
- wǒ shì xué shēng - I am a student
- wǒ shì lǎo shī - I am a teacher
- wǒ shì yī shēng - I am a doctor
- wǒ shì gōng chéng shī - I am an engineer
- wǒ shì jǐng chá - I am a police officer
- wǒ zuò ruǎn jiàn gōng zuò - I work in software
- tā bú shì xué shēng - He/She is not a student

## How Long Working/Studying (gōng zuò/xué xí duō jiǔ)

### Questions:
- nǐ gōng zuò duō jiǔ le? - How long have you been working?
- nǐ xué zhōng wén duō jiǔ le? - How long have you been learning Chinese?
- tā zài zhè lǐ gōng zuò duō jiǔ le? - How long has he/she been working here?
- nǐ zuò lǎo shī duō jiǔ le? - How long have you been a teacher?

### Answers:
- wǒ gōng zuò sān nián le - I have been working for three years
- wǒ gōng zuò wǔ nián le - I have been working for five years
- wǒ xué zhōng wén liǎng gè yuè le - I have been learning Chinese for two months
- tā gōng zuò shí nián le - He/She has been working for ten years
- wǒ zuò lǎo shī yī nián le - I have been a teacher for one year

## Date of Birth (shēng rì)

### Questions:
- nǐ de shēng rì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào? - When is your birthday? (month and date)
- nǐ de shēng rì shì shén me shí hou? - When is your birthday?
- tā de shēng rì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào? - When is his/her birthday?

### Answers:
- wǒ de shēng rì shì sān yuè wǔ hào - My birthday is March 5th
- wǒ de shēng rì shì shí yuè èr shí hào - My birthday is October 20th
- tā de shēng rì shì liù yuè shí wǔ hào - His/Her birthday is June 15th
