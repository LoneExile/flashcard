# Abilities Q&A

## Can You Speak Chinese (huì shuō zhōng wén)

### Questions:
- nǐ huì shuō zhōng wén ma? - Can you speak Chinese?
- tā huì shuō zhōng wén ma? - Can he/she speak Chinese?
- nǐ de lǎo shī huì shuō zhōng wén ma? - Can your teacher speak Chinese?
- nǐ de péng yǒu huì shuō zhōng wén ma? - Can your friend speak Chinese?
- nǐ huì bú huì shuō zhōng wén? - Can you speak Chinese or not?

### Answers:
- wǒ huì shuō zhōng wén - I can speak Chinese
- wǒ bú huì shuō zhōng wén - I can't speak Chinese
- wǒ huì shuō yì diǎn zhōng wén - I can speak a little Chinese
- tā huì shuō zhōng wén - He/She can speak Chinese
- tā bú huì shuō zhōng wén - He/She can't speak Chinese
- wǒ de lǎo shī huì shuō zhōng wén - My teacher can speak Chinese

## Where Do You Learn (zài nǎ lǐ xué)

### Questions:
- nǐ zài nǎ lǐ xué zhōng wén? - Where do you learn Chinese?
- nǐ shì zài nǎ lǐ xué de zhōng wén? - Where did you learn Chinese?
- tā zài nǎ lǐ xué zhōng wén? - Where does he/she learn Chinese?
- tā shì zài nǎ lǐ xué de kāi chē? - Where did he/she learn to drive?
- nǐ zài nǎ lǐ xué zuò cài? - Where do you learn to cook?

### Answers:
- wǒ zài xué xiào xué zhōng wén - I learn Chinese at school
- wǒ zài wǎng shàng xué zhōng wén - I learn Chinese online
- wǒ shì zài wǎng shàng xué de zhōng wén - I learned Chinese online
- tā zài dà xué xué zhōng wén - He/She learns Chinese at university
- wǒ zài jiā xué zuò cài - I learn to cook at home
- wǒ shì zài běi jīng xué de zhōng wén - I learned Chinese in Beijing

## How Long Have You Been Learning (xué duō jiǔ)

### Questions:
- nǐ xué zhōng wén duō jiǔ le? - How long have you been learning Chinese?
- tā xué zhōng wén duō jiǔ le? - How long has he/she been learning Chinese?
- nǐ xué kāi chē duō jiǔ le? - How long have you been learning to drive?
- nǐ xué zuò cài duō jiǔ le? - How long have you been learning to cook?

### Answers:
- wǒ xué zhōng wén sān gè yuè le - I have been learning Chinese for three months
- wǒ xué zhōng wén yī nián le - I have been learning Chinese for one year
- wǒ xué zhōng wén liǎng nián le - I have been learning Chinese for two years
- tā xué zhōng wén wǔ nián le - He/She has been learning Chinese for five years
- wǒ xué kāi chē liù gè yuè le - I have been learning to drive for six months
- wǒ xué zuò cài sān nián le - I have been learning to cook for three years

## When Did You Start Learning (shén me shí hou kāi shǐ xué)

### Questions:
- nǐ shén me shí hou kāi shǐ xué zhōng wén? - When did you start learning Chinese?
- tā shén me shí hou kāi shǐ xué zhōng wén? - When did he/she start learning Chinese?
- nǐ shén me shí hou kāi shǐ xué kāi chē? - When did you start learning to drive?

### Answers:
- wǒ shàng gè yuè kāi shǐ xué zhōng wén - I started learning Chinese last month
- wǒ qù nián kāi shǐ xué zhōng wén - I started learning Chinese last year
- tā jīn nián kāi shǐ xué zhōng wén - He/She started learning Chinese this year
- wǒ sān nián qián kāi shǐ xué zhōng wén - I started learning Chinese three years ago

## General Abilities

### Questions:
- nǐ huì shén me? - What can you do?
- tā huì zuò shén me? - What can he/she do?
- nǐ hái huì shén me? - What else can you do?

### Answers:
- wǒ huì shuō zhōng wén - I can speak Chinese
- wǒ huì zuò zhōng guó cài - I can cook Chinese food
- wǒ huì kāi chē - I can drive
- wǒ yě huì shuō yīng wén - I can also speak English
- tā huì zuò hěn duō cài - He/She can cook many dishes

## Ability Level

### Questions:
- nǐ zhōng wén zěn me yàng? - How is your Chinese?
- tā de zhōng wén zěn me yàng? - How is his/her Chinese?
- nǐ zuò de cài zěn me yàng? - How is your cooking?

### Answers:
- wǒ de zhōng wén bù tài hǎo - My Chinese is not very good
- wǒ de zhōng wén hái kě yǐ - My Chinese is okay
- tā de zhōng wén fēi cháng hǎo - His/Her Chinese is very good
- wǒ zuò de cài hěn hǎo chī - My cooking is delicious

## Teaching Others

### Questions:
- nǐ jiāo shéi zhōng wén? - Who do you teach Chinese to?
- shéi jiāo nǐ zhōng wén? - Who teaches you Chinese?
- nǐ de lǎo shī jiāo shén me? - What does your teacher teach?

### Answers:

- tā zài wǎng shàng xué zhōng wén ma? - Does he/she learn Chinese online?

### Answers:
- wǒ zài wǎng shàng xué zhōng wén - I learn Chinese online
- wǒ shì zài wǎng shàng xué de - I learned it online
- tā yě zài wǎng shàng xué zhōng wén - He/She also learns Chinese online
